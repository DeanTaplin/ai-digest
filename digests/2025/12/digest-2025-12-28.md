### NVIDIA Releases NitroGen: Vision-Action Model for Gaming Agents
**Source:** [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/12/28/nvidia-ai-researchers-release-nitrogen-an-open-vision-action-foundation-model-for-generalist-gaming-agents/)

**Key Insight:** Foundation models for embodied agents are emerging, trained on massive behavioral datasets that span multiple domains (games).

**Why It Matters:** Gaming agents serve as testbeds for general autonomous AI. Open models and datasets accelerate research into vision-to-action systems applicable beyond gaming.
