**Source:** arXiv (cs.AI) | **Published:** Feb 17, 2026

Security researchers demonstrate a novel attack vector against the orchestrator multi-agent pattern ‚Äî a single indirect prompt injection can cascade across multiple sub-agents to leak sensitive data, even when data access controls are in place.
